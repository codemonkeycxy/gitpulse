② Project Momentum team velocity over time

Y-axis = commits per month. Trend (↑/↓/→) compares the last 3 months to the 3 months before that.

③ Contributor Distribution knowledge concentration

Slice size = share of total commits. A dominant slice means knowledge is concentrated — if that person left, much of the commit history context leaves with them.

④ Knowledge Silos files touched by only one person

Bar = number of files that person alone touched in the window. Expand to browse individual files.

⑤ Firefighting Frequency reverts, hotfixes & rollbacks over time

Commits whose subject line starts with revert, hotfix, or rollback. Y-axis = count per month. Larger dots mark spike months (2× the average).
